Achievements of TEDx VITPune

Celebrating milestones and initiatives that have propelled our organization ahead

Achievements

TEDx-VITPune has achieved significant milestones in various domains. These achievements stand as a testament to our organization's progress and resilience.

2015
Inception of TEDxVITPune

TEDxVITPune was founded, becoming one of the earliest TEDx events in Pune.

2016
Diverse Theme: “Let’s Cross Over”

Focused on stepping out of comfort zones and embracing new challenges.

2017-23
Consistent Annual Events

Successfully hosted events each year with thought-provoking themes and a growing audience.

2019
Theme: “Unravelling the Parallax” & Innovative Rotating Stage

Encouraged viewing ideas from multiple angles; featured a mechanically designed rotating stage to reflect the concept.

2020-23
Community Conversations Launched

Interactive discussions on culture, cinema, literature, and more to engage the local community outside the main events.

All Years
Platform for Diverse Speakers

Speakers from tech, art, science, literature, entrepreneurship, and social activism shared their stories.
