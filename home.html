About Us

TEDxVITPune is an independently organized TEDx event hosted by Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune. It serves as a platform for thinkers, innovators, and changemakers to share powerful ideas that inspire action. Aligned with TED’s mission of "Ideas Worth Spreading," TEDxVITPune aims to spark conversations and foster a culture of curiosity and innovation within the local community.

Our Journey

TEDxVITPune has successfully hosted 9 impactful editions with themes ranging from "Connecting the Dots" to "The Space Between", featuring over 50+ inspiring speakers and engaging thousands of attendees.

Ideas That Echo Beyond the Event

We believe in the transformative power of ideas and the ability of individuals to bring meaningful change—starting with a spark, a story, and a shared space for dialogue.
