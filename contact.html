Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


TEDxVITPune is locally organized under TED guidelines, whereas TED is a global conference organized by TED itself.
Anyone with an interest in ideas and innovation is welcome to attend, including students, professionals, and the general public.
TEDxVITPune hosts one major annual event and may also conduct smaller sessions or workshops throughout the year.
Each event is centered around a unique theme that encourages diverse and meaningful discussions.
You can find our past talks on the TEDx YouTube channel or our official website.
Some events may have a nominal fee to cover costs, but many are free or subsidized for students.
